## Detailed Description
AIXORD Companion brings structured governance to your AI-assisted projects — for free.

Whether you're using ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Gemini, or any other AI tool, AIXORD Companion gives you:

**Structured Phases**: DISCOVER > BRAINSTORM > PLAN > BLUEPRINT > EXECUTE > REVIEW
Move through your project with clear phases. Know where you are, what comes next, and what's required.

**Quality Gates**: 17 governance gates with human-readable descriptions
Check off gates as you progress. Track which requirements are met and which need attention.

**Universal Prompts**: Phase-specific prompt templates, copy-to-clipboard
Get the right prompt for your current phase. Works with any AI tool — just paste and go.

**AI Tool Detection**: Automatically detects ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more
The extension knows which AI tool you're using and adapts accordingly.

**Decision Ledger**: Log decisions with rationale and timestamps
Build an audit trail of your project decisions. Export as Markdown for team sharing.

**One-Click Reports**: Export your full project governance report as Markdown
Share with your team, attach to PRs, or archive for compliance.

**100% Local**: No account required. No data leaves your browser.
Everything is stored locally in your browser. No backend, no tracking, no sign-up.
